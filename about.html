ABOUT US

welcome to nexatel

Nexatel broadband is an internet broadband service provider in Mathura (Category B ISP license holder), and a leading competitor in the telecommunications market.

We are committed to making it easy for everyone, including those living in the remotest areas of the city, to access reliable, secure and affordable broadband connectivity.

At Nexatel, uniqueness is in our DNA. We understand that every business is unique and have helped nations most prestigious brands run their business efficiently with our customised IT and Telecom solutions.

Leading the way in digital infrastructure

In the year 2000, during the dot-com revolution, Nexatel was founded in Mathura. Today, it stands as a formidable force in the Information Technology and Telecom sectors with a strong pan-India presence.

Our Vision

To create cutting-edge digital platforms for a seamless technology-driven world.

Our Mission

  • To attain leadership by achieving customer delight by virtue of service delivery, curated technology solutions derived through strong technology alliances and innovative insights.

  • To provide reliable and secure services that will connect our customers wherever they want. To create an environment that nurtures employee creativity and learning, thus creating career roadmaps within the organisation.

  • To contribute to society in terms of time and resources, creating a positive impact and improving livelihoods.

  • To be recognised as a complete system-driven and process-oriented organisation for providing a consistent and positive customer experience across domains and geographies.
